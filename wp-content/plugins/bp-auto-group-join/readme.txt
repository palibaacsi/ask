Automatically join BuddyPress members to Groups.

== Description ==

Automatically join new and existing BuddyPress members to Groups of your choice. Optionally determine which group to join based on Member Type, using our [BuddyPress Member Types](http://www.buddyboss.com/product/buddypress-member-types/) plugin.

Watch our [video tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IN9iJIFRyts) for setup and configuration instructions.

BP Auto Group Join is built by the experienced developers at BuddyBoss who also offer premium [BuddyPress themes](http://www.buddyboss.com/themes/ "BuddyPress themes from BuddyBoss") and [plugins](http://www.buddyboss.com/plugins/ "BuddyPress plugins from BuddyBoss") to build your social network.

== Installation ==

= From your WordPress dashboard =

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
2. Search for 'BP Auto Group Join'
3. Activate BP Auto Group Join from your Plugins page.

= From WordPress.org =

1. Download BP Auto Group Join.
2. Upload the 'bp-auto-group-join' directory to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, etc...)
3. Activate BP Auto Group Join from your Plugins page.

= Configuration =

1. Visit 'BuddyBoss > Auto Group Join' for options and support.
2. Edit any BuddyPress group from the admin to select auto-join options.
3. Optionally configure Member Types using our [BuddyPress Member Types](http://www.buddyboss.com/product/buddypress-member-types/) plugin.
